Collaborative GPS: Strength in Numbers

More phones together = better accuracy for everyone

How It Works

GPS accuracy varies wildly at festivals. Tall stages, dense crowds, and metal structures all degrade signals. FestivAir solves this by combining GPS data from your entire squad.

Weighted Averaging: Members with strong GPS (5m accuracy) contribute more to the group position than those with weak signals (50m accuracy). The math gives you a group center more accurate than any single phone.

Find Your Group: One tap shows you exactly where your squad is clustered. See how spread out everyone is and the precise distance to get back to the group.

Mesh as Distance: If you're connected via Bluetooth, you're within 10 meters. The mesh connection itself acts as a proximity signal even when GPS fails.
