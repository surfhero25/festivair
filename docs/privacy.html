Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

FestivAir ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Data

We collect your location data to show your position on the squad map and share it with your squad members. This includes:

  • GPS coordinates when the app is in use
  • Background location (if you enable it) to keep your squad updated
  • Location accuracy and timestamp
Important: Your location is only shared with members of squads you join. We do not sell or share your location data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Profile Information

  • Display name you choose
  • Profile icon/emoji
  • Optional: profile photo, bio, social media handles

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • Battery level (to help coordinate mesh networking)
  • Bluetooth connectivity status

How We Use Your Information

  • Squad Features: Share your location with squad members so you can find each other
  • Mesh Networking: Enable device-to-device communication when cell service is unavailable
  • Notifications: Send alerts about set times and squad activity
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve the app (anonymized)

Mesh Network & Data Relay

FestivAir uses Bluetooth mesh networking to help users communicate when cell service is limited. Here's how it works:

  • Your device may relay encrypted data packets for other FestivAir users nearby
  • All relayed data is encrypted - your device cannot read other squads' messages
  • This relay happens automatically in the background to improve network coverage for everyone
  • No personally identifiable information from other users is stored on your device
Encryption: All squad communications are encrypted. When your device relays data for other squads, it cannot decrypt or read that data - it simply passes it along.

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Your profile and squad data is stored on your device
  • iCloud: Data syncs via iCloud when you have internet connectivity
  • Retention: Location data older than 24 hours is automatically deleted

Data Sharing

We share your information only in these circumstances:

  • With Your Squad: Squad members see your name, icon, and location
  • Party Hosts: If you request to join a party, the host sees your profile
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect safety

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data
  • Share your location with advertisers
  • Use your data for targeted advertising

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all data we have about you in the app
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data anytime
  • Location Control: Disable location sharing in iOS Settings
  • Opt Out: Leave any squad to stop sharing your location with them

Children's Privacy

FestivAir is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
