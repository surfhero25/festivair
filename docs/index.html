Good to Know

Honest details about how the app works

Bluetooth Range

Bluetooth connections work within about 30 feet in ideal conditions. Crowds, bodies, and obstacles reduce this range. You need to be relatively close for direct phone-to-phone sharing.

GPS Accuracy

GPS accuracy varies from 10-50 feet depending on conditions. Tall stages, dense crowds, and buildings can degrade signals. The map shows approximate locations, not exact positions.

Battery Impact

Using GPS and Bluetooth does use battery. The app tries to be efficient, but expect some battery drain during use. Bring a portable charger to festivals.

iPhone Only

FestivAir currently only works on iPhone with iOS 17 or later. Android support is not available. All squad members need iPhones.

Squad Size

Squads work best with up to 12 people. Larger groups may experience slower updates and more battery drain from managing many connections.

No Cell? Limited Updates

Without cell service, location sharing only works via Bluetooth when nearby. If your group spreads out beyond Bluetooth range with no signal, locations won't update until someone gets service.
